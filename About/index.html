Where Beachfront Dreams Come True

Teliza Beach Gardens has been creating magical moments on the pristine shores of Ruiru for over a decade. Our commitment to excellence and attention to detail ensures that every event is truly special.

Located on one of Kenya's most beautiful beaches, we provide the perfect setting for weddings, corporate events, and special celebrations. With stunning ocean views, beautifully manicured gardens, and world-class facilities, we bring your vision to life.
