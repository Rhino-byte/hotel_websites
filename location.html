Getting Here is Easy

Simple, step‑by‑step directions from Ruiru Toll on Thika Road

Exit at Ruiru Toll (A2)

On Thika Road (A2), take the Ruiru Toll exit. Keep right towards Ruiru town as you leave the toll area.

Head towards Twiga Dam

Drive via the Kamiti/Ruiru exit road and continue towards Twiga Dam (Ruiru area). Stay on the main access road.

Follow Signs

Follow signs for Twiga Dam / Teliza Beach Gardens. If unsure, ask locals for "Teliza Beach Gardens" near the dam.

Main Gate Arrival

Proceed along the dam access road to our main gate. Security will guide you to parking and your event area.
