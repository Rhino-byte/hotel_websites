Weddings by the Water

Plan Your Wedding

Exchange your vows on lush lawns kissed by the light off Ruiru Dam, then flow into a beautifully styled reception beneath the open sky. Our planning team choreographs every detail—from the aisle walk to the first dance—so your day feels effortless. Guests enjoy intuitive service, spacious layouts, and countless photo‑perfect moments at golden hour. Whether intimate or grand, your celebration remains personal, elegant, and unforgettable.

Graduations & Milestones

Host Your Party

Mark academic achievements with a vibrant celebration that brings everyone together. Our lawns and pavilions flex for speeches, slideshow highlights, and big group photos with family and friends. Menus are tailored for every age group—from hearty mains to sweet finishes and refreshing drinks. With upbeat music and an easygoing waterfront vibe, your graduation becomes a milestone everyone remembers.

Corporate Meetings & Retreats

Plan a Corporate Event

Focus deeply in calm, professional spaces designed for real outcomes. Our conference hall pairs dependable AV and high‑speed Wi‑Fi with flexible seating for plenaries and breakouts. Keep energy high with well‑timed coffee breaks and curated menus that don’t slow momentum. After sessions, teams reconnect by the water, where fresh air helps great ideas stick.

Kids & Fun Activities

Plan Family Fun

Let kids be kids with simple outdoor fun that feels easy for parents too. Splash sessions at the pool, lawn games, and peaceful boat rides keep little ones entertained and teens engaged. Photo‑ready moments pop up everywhere, from bright afternoons to golden sunsets. It’s joyful, screen‑free time that brings all generations together.

Catering & Cuisine

Explore Menus

Delight guests with cuisine that matches your moment—polished plated courses, interactive live stations, or abundant buffets. Every dish is crafted with flavor, freshness, and thoughtful presentation. We tailor menus to your theme, season, and preferences, with vegetarian and kid‑friendly options ready. The result is dining that feels generous, memorable, and distinctly yours.
