Teliza Beach Gardens Location

Discover the charm of Teliza Beach Gardens, a serene escape set beside the calm waters of Ruiru Dam. With beautiful gardens, relaxing boat rides, a refreshing swimming pool, and spacious event grounds, it’s the ideal place to celebrate, unwind, and connect with loved ones. Whether it’s a family outing, graduation party, or a weekend getaway, Teliza Beach offers something special for everyone.
